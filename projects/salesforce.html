Salesforce Sales & Service Cloud Implementation

Delivered a Salesforce CRM implementation that transformed fragmented sales and customer service operations into a centralized cloud-based platform. Explore Case Study Final Project

The Transformation Story

BTS Motors experienced rapid growth that exposed limitations in its spreadsheet-driven sales and customer service operations. Sales opportunities, customer information, and service requests were managed through disconnected manual processes, reducing visibility, delaying response times, and limiting collaboration across departments. This engagement focused on implementing Salesforce Sales Cloud and Service Cloud to centralize customer data, automate key business processes, improve service delivery, strengthen security, and provide leadership with real-time operational visibility.
