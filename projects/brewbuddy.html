Case Study • Business Process Improvement

BrewBuddy

Morning Service Operations Redesign

A Business Analysis case study focused on reducing wait times, improving order accuracy, standardizing workflows, and improving customer experience during peak morning demand.

Project Overview

BrewBuddy was experiencing operational pressure during peak morning hours. Long queues, frequent order mistakes, rushed drink preparation, and inconsistent workflows were affecting customer satisfaction and increasing the risk of losing loyal customers to a nearby competitor.

Business Problem

Long customer wait times
Frequent order mistakes
Rushed drink preparation
Poor peak-hour coordination
Inconsistent service quality
Risk of customer loss

Project Objectives

Reduce average wait time
Improve order accuracy
Standardize service workflows
Improve staff coordination
Increase customer satisfaction
Support peak-hour demand

Recommended Solution

The recommended solution is a Business Process Improvement initiative supported by clearer staff roles, standardized order-taking and drink-preparation workflows, digital order management, and performance tracking through operational KPIs.
