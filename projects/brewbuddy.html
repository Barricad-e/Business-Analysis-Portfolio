BrewBuddy Business Process Improvement

Case Study

A business analysis project focused on reducing customer wait times, improving order accuracy, and redesigning morning service operations.

Business Problem

BrewBuddy experiences long customer wait times, frequent order mistakes, rushed drink preparation, and declining customer satisfaction during peak morning hours. These issues affect service quality and create a risk of losing loyal customers to nearby competitors.

Project Objective

The objective of this project is to improve BrewBuddy's order fulfilment and customer service process by standardizing workflows, reducing delays, improving order accuracy, and supporting better staff coordination during peak demand periods.

Recommended Solution

The recommended solution is a Business Process Improvement project supported by digital order management, clearer staff roles, standardized drink preparation steps, and performance tracking through key operational metrics.
