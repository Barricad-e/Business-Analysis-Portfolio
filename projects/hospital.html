Pure Life Hospital

Healthcare Analytics & Business Intelligence

Executive Summary

Developed an end-to-end healthcare analytics solution using Microsoft Power BI to transform healthcare transaction data into interactive executive dashboards. The solution combined data preparation, data modeling, DAX calculations, KPI development, and visual analytics to improve visibility into financial performance, patient activity, operational efficiency, and clinical outcomes, enabling evidence-based decision-making across the organization.

Executive Dashboards

The Power BI solution consists of two interactive dashboards designed for executive reporting. The first focuses on hospital performance, patient flow, and operational KPIs, while the second provides detailed financial and revenue analysis to support strategic decision-making.

Business Value

The dashboard provides executives with a centralized view of financial, operational, and clinical performance. Interactive reporting enables faster decision-making through real-time KPI monitoring, revenue analysis, patient trend analysis, specialty performance evaluation, and resource utilization insights.
