Pure Life Hospital

Healthcare Analytics & Business Intelligence

Developed a healthcare business intelligence solution using Microsoft Power BI to transform hospital transaction data into interactive executive dashboards. The project focused on analyzing financial performance, patient activity, specialty demand, and operational KPIs to provide leadership with timely insights that support strategic planning, resource optimization, and data-driven decision-making.

The Business Story

Pure Life Hospital required better visibility into the relationship between patient activity, clinical outcomes, doctor performance, specialty demand, and financial results. Existing reporting did not provide a centralized view of operational and revenue performance.

This case study shows how Power BI was used to transform healthcare transaction data into interactive dashboards that support executive monitoring, revenue analysis, patient flow review, and data-driven healthcare operations.

Executive Summary

Delivered a healthcare business intelligence solution using Microsoft Power BI to transform hospital transaction data into executive dashboards, financial KPIs, patient activity insights, and clinical performance reporting. The solution improved visibility into revenue performance, patient flow, doctor and specialty activity, and operational decision-making.
