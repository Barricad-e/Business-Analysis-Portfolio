BikeGear Solutions

Sales Intelligence & Business Analytics

Delivered a business intelligence and sales analytics solution that transformed historical retail sales data into actionable insights for executive decision-making. Leveraging Microsoft Excel, the engagement encompassed data preparation, exploratory data analysis (EDA), KPI development, customer segmentation, regional and product performance analysis, profitability analysis, interactive dashboard design, and executive reporting. The resulting insights informed strategic recommendations to optimize revenue growth, inventory planning, demand forecasting, marketing effectiveness, and operational decision-making.

The Business Story

BikeGear Solutions sought to improve business performance by gaining deeper visibility into retail sales trends, customer behaviour, regional performance, and product profitability. Existing reporting methods provided limited analytical insight, making it difficult for leadership to make timely, data-driven decisions.

This case study demonstrates how business analysis and Microsoft Excel were used to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights through data preparation, exploratory analysis, KPI development, dashboard creation, and executive recommendations.

Business Overview

BikeGear Solutions is a multinational retailer specializing in bike racks and cycling accessories across Canada, the United States, and Australia.
